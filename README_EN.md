## About

This project systematically integrates the core knowledge of biochemistry and molecular biology, covering **154 key skills** across 16 categories:

| Category | Skills | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| 🧬 Molecular Biology & Gene Expression | 16 | DNA/RNA structure, transcription, splicing, modification |
| 🔧 Genetic Engineering & DNA Technology | 8 | Cloning vectors, restriction enzymes, CRISPR, expression systems |
| 🧪 Genomics & Disease Gene Analysis | 8 | GWAS, positional cloning, exome sequencing, gene chips |
| 🏗️ Protein Structure & Function | 7 | Primary structure, α-helix, conformational disease, phosphorylation |
| ⚗️ Metabolic Pathways & Regulation | 15 | Glycolysis, fatty acid oxidation, ketone bodies, ethanol metabolism |
| 🧫 Nucleic Acid & Nucleotide Metabolism | 7 | Purine/pyrimidine synthesis & degradation, antimetabolites |
| 🫧 Lipid & Lipoprotein Metabolism | 9 | LDL, HDL, bile acids, lipoprotein classification |
| 📡 Signal Transduction & Cell Regulation | 10 | GPCR, ion channels, p53, PTEN, RB |
| 🧬 Cancer Biology & Precision Medicine | 7 | Oncogenes, multistage carcinogenesis, precision medicine |
| 🏥 Clinical Biochemistry & Diagnosis | 9 | OGTT, HbA1c, jaundice differential, dehydration |
| 🔬 Enzymology & Separation Techniques | 6 | Gel filtration, ion exchange, P450, ribozymes |
| 🦠 Microbiology & Translation Mechanisms | 6 | Initiation, elongation, SRP, antibiotics |
| ⚡ Redox & Energy Metabolism | 5 | Respiratory chain, oxidative phosphorylation, P/O ratio |
| 🏋️ Endocrinology & Trace Elements | 4 | Iodine dual function, exercise physiology, epinephrine |
| 📚 Medical Textbooks & Digital Resources | 6 | Revision principles, digital textbooks, metadata |
| 🌟 Other Featured Skills | 31 | Telomerase, PCR, miRNA, histone modification, etc. |

**Target Audience**: Medical students, biomedical researchers, clinicians, biochemistry educators, pharmacy & life science professionals

**Reference Textbook**: PMPH *Biochemistry and Molecular Biology* 10th Edition (ISBN: 978-7-117-36618-8)

**⚠️ Risk ⚠️**: This skill collection is compiled from textbook content for educational reference only. Any clinical diagnostic or therapeutic decisions must be reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals.

## Quick Start

### Installation

```bash
openclaw skills install biochemistry-molecular-biology-pmph-10edition
```

### Usage

Each skill contains four sections:
1. **Trigger** — When to invoke this skill
2. **Procedure** — Standardized workflow
3. **Precautions** — Contraindications & warnings
4. **References** — Supporting documentation

### Query Examples

**Concept explanation:**
```bash
Explain the regulatory mechanism of PFK-1 in glycolysis
```

**Clinical case analysis:**
```bash
Analyze the molecular mechanism of familial hypercholesterolemia caused by LDL receptor defects
```

**Experimental design:**
```bash
Design an experimental protocol for expressing human recombinant protein in E. coli
```
